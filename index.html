§06WHY IT WORKS

Three things we
do differently.

The reasons placements stay, members renew, and other founders keep telling each other about us.

— Principle 01

Built around specialization.

Remote global talent placement is our core business, not a side hustle bolted onto a domestic agency. The whole company is built around it. Sourcing infrastructure, training systems, candidate pipelines, all of it.

It's the only thing we do, so we do it differently.

— Principle 02

Curation over volume.

You get a short list of the right people, not a flood of unqualified applicants. We screen hundreds so you spend your time on the ones worth meeting. Less noise, faster decisions, better matches.

Volume is for jobs boards. Curation is for hiring.

— Principle 03

Both sides ready to work.

We prep the candidate on U.S. business culture, communication norms, and async collaboration. We prep you on the integration plan, the milestones, the early signals to watch for.

Less friction week one. Faster ramp by month three.
