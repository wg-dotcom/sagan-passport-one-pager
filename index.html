What Passport actually is

Not a recruiter. A force multiplier for your hiring.

We screen hundreds of candidates so you only meet the best. The people we place are full-time integrated contractors, not markup agencies or freelancers.

Passport is built for founders and operators who need real leverage on their team but don't want to compromise on quality. Your local talent budget keeps shrinking. The work doesn't. We bridge that gap with vetted global talent who can sit inside your operation full-time, at a rate that lets you actually scale.

Every candidate goes through direct sourcing, live English and communication interviews, U.S. business culture alignment, role-specific experience validation, and skills assessments or portfolio reviews when the role calls for it. You see a curated shortlist with resume, video intro, strengths assessment, and recruiter evaluation for each person. No resume sifting on your end.
